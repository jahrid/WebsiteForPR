History of My Messiah School of Cavite, Inc.

Our Foundation

My Messiah School of Cavite Inc. (MMSC) is a non-stock, non-profit educational institution duly registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission. The name of the school was carefully chosen to convey a meaningful message to the community — a commitment to Christ-centered education and holistic formation.

Founded in 2009, the school began with a modest enrollment of 200 students. It was first established near the NHA Compound in Poblacion 1, Municipality of GMA, Cavite. From the beginning, MMSC was built on strong Christian values and a dedication to academic excellence.

Early Years

The Main Campus in Poblacion 1, GMA, Cavite initially offered Pre-Elementary, Elementary, and Secondary levels. It was recognized by the Department of Education (DepEd) under the K–12 Curriculum.

Guided by the biblical principle:

  • “The fear of the Lord is the beginning of wisdom, and knowledge of the Holy One is understanding.” — Proverbs 9:10

    • the school committed itself to developing students who are God-fearing, academically competent, and morally upright citizens prepared for a fast-changing world.

    Growth and Development

    The School Year 2015–2016 marked a significant milestone for MMSC. During this fruitful year, the school expanded by establishing a Senior High School Campus located in Delas Alas, GMA, Cavite. The Senior High School was recognized by DepEd in the same year.

    The school began offering various academic strands and technical-vocational specializations, including:

  • ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management)
  • GAS (General Academic Strand)
  • HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences)
  • STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics)
  • ICT (Information and Communications Technology)
  • HE (Home Economics)

    • Our Mission Today

    Today, My Messiah School of Cavite continues to pursue its vision of becoming a model institution that forms well-trained students who are spiritually grounded, academically prepared, and morally responsible.

    Through its Christ-centered education and commitment to excellence, MMSC nurtures learners to become productive citizens equipped with knowledge, skills, and values necessary to thrive in today’s dynamic society.

    Accreditation and Recognition

    Both campuses’ Junior High School levels are accredited by the Private Education Assistance Committee (PEAC). As a result, almost all junior high school students are beneficiaries of the ESC (Education Service Contracting) Program, receiving tuition subsidies that make quality private education more accessible to families in the community.

    MMSC remains duly recognized by the Department of Education and continues to uphold the standards required for delivering quality K–12 education in the Philippines.
